Author and Illustrator

Robert

Robert Mitchell is the creator of Kat and Jat. A self-taught storyteller from Wyandanch, New York, his passion for writing became apparent at a young age when he would spend summers at the local library. He joined book clubs, and his work gained recognition when he won a local writing contest in 1978, making headlines in the local newspaper.

Robert earned his degree in Media and Communications from SUNY Old Westbury and worked as a daily newspaper journalist for two decades in New York, New Jersey, and Washington, D.C., where he covered topics ranging from business and technology to the federal government. He co-authored "Inside Afghanistan," an illustrated book depicting everyday life in the military, and recently published his first screenplay, "A Single Vibration," which was performed at the Hollywood Fringe Festival in 2023. The screenplay delves into a soldier's experience after the war.
